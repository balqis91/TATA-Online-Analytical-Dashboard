#  TATA Online Analytical Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyse sales performance, profitability, and product trends across different regions and categories.  

The goal is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making and highlight key business performance drivers.

##  Key Insights
- Digital products generate over 70% of total profit, making them the most valuable category  
- Merchandise contributes significantly to sales volume but delivers lower profit margins  
- Certain regions consistently outperform others in terms of profitability  
- Monthly profit trends show steady growth, indicating strong business performance over time  

---

##  Features of the Dashboard
- Interactive filters for category and region analysis  
- KPI cards displaying key metrics such as total profit and sales  
- Time-series analysis of profit trends  
- Geographical visualisation of performance across locations  
- Comparative analysis between product categories  

---

##  Business Value
This dashboard helps stakeholders:
- Identify high-performing products and regions  
- Understand profit distribution across categories  
- Make data-driven decisions to optimise sales strategy  
- Monitor performance trends over time
